Situated just a short 2 minute walk from the Grand Pier in Weston Super Mare, Allstars is easy to find and impossible to miss!

Live Sport And Entertainment: Here at Allstars Weston-Super-Mare, we have the facilities to show up to 7 events at any given time in HD. We have 2 large projected HD screens as well as over 20 large screen wall-mounted LCD TVs that you can request your choice of broadcast. We also have BT Sports HD and Sky HD across all of our large HD TVs and HD projected screens. To supplement those we have access to many European channels, showing games from across the continent and the world as well as home matches. Some of these show games and sports that you just won't find on a normal home Satellite box. • Over 20 wall mounted HD TVs • 2 x large screen HD projectors • Sky Sports HD • BT Sport HD • Eurosport HD

Choose Your Channel: Don't think you have to watch what everyone else is watching. If you're playing at any of our 18 tables you can request the channel of your choice on your nearest TV. Want to watch live Formula One? No problem. Football? You tell us which match and we'll hook your set up.

Pool, Snooker and More Allstars Weston sports: • 3 full size Snooker tables • 2 Sam K Steel American Pool tables • 12 Supreme Winner English Pool tables • Every table uses state of the art 450watt Luminaire low profile lights for unrivalled lighting quality •1 Joy Chinese 8ball table • Poker Machine • Boxing Machine • 4 Dart Boards with electronic scoreboards • Table Football • Juke Box Day visitors can still take advantage of our competitive table rates: Tables available at an hourly rate or If you just want a few quick frames of english pool then one of our two coin-operated English Pool tables may be just what you need. just make sure you don't pot the 8 ball on the break!

Food available all day Allstars Sports Bars offer a full menu all day

Smoking Area: Allstars Weston offers a comfortable area for those who fancy a cigarette.

Cues and Accessories: Allstars Weston has a great range of cues and accessories available. If you're looking for a new cue, where better as you can try before you buy? Just ask a member of the Allstars team for assistance.

Amusements: Every Allstars venue has amusements to keep you entertained. Including Bar Football, Boxing Machines, Fruit Machines and Amusement only Machines. For our regulars, Cue Lockers are available for hire.
